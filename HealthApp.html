Stage app

Role: UX Research, UI Design Date: Dec 2014, Team Size: 5

Objective

A project to understand how we could impart health awareness and education to millennials.

Overview

Videos available on most platforms today, lack context. One needs to watch the video to know if it was worth watching at all. New videos are difficult to discover as well. Stage tries to tackle these issues.
